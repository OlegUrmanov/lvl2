Paris Destination

It is the capital and most populous city in France, with an area of ​​105 square kilometers (41 square miles) and a population of 2,206,488. With 200,000 inhabitants in 1328, Paris, then the capital of France, was the most populous city in Europe
For comparison, in London in 1300 there were 80,000 inhabitants. Since the 17th century, Paris has been one of the largest centers of Europe, commerce, fashion, science, music and painting. In the Paris region, there was a GDP of 681 billion euros ($ 850 billion) at 2016, accounting for 31 percent of France's GDP.
In 2013-2014, the Paris region had the third largest GDP in the world and the largest regional GDP in the EU. According to a study by the Economist Intelligence Unit Worldwide Cost of Living Survey in 2018, Paris was the second largest expensive city in the world, behind Singapore and ahead of Zurich, Hong Kong, Oslo and Geneva.
